POLICIES

Hair Preparation

Clients are required to arrive with clean and detangled hair. Blow-drying is optional, but it can help save time during your appointment. An additional charge of $10 will apply if the hair is not detangled upon arrival.

Punctuality

Please arrive on time for your appointment. Appointments will be automatically terminated if you are more than 1 hour late. If you are 30 minutes late, an extra $10 will be added to your bill. Kindly ensure you plan for possible delays to respect your appointment time.

Evening Appointments

For evening bookings, please choose styles that can reasonably be completed by 10:00 PM. Quicker styles such as cornrows or medium mid-back braids are recommended for these times. Longer or more time-intensive styles (e.g., small braids, boho braids, waist-length braids) should be booked earlier in the day to ensure enough time for quality service.

Booking in Advance

To guarantee your desired time, please book at least 7 days in advance. Last-minute appointments are subject to availability and may not be guaranteed.

Deposit Requirement

A non-refundable deposit of $35 is required to secure your appointment. If the client is booking french curl braids, the deposit fee is $55. This deposit will be deducted from your total cost on the day of your appointment. Failure to pay the deposit may result in cancellation of the appointment without prior notice.

Cancellation Policy

If you need to cancel, please notify me at least 24 hours before your appointment to avoid a cancellation fee.

Extension Preparation

If your extensions are not pre-stretched, an additional charge of $20 will apply for the stretching service. Please ensure the extensions are ready to avoid any delays or extra charges.

Sizing Assistance

If you need clarification regarding the size, length, or style of your braids, feel free to DM me for assistance before your appointment. I'm here to help ensure you get the exact style you want!

Hair Provision

Hair is included in all prices listed. If you prefer to provide your own extensions, they must be dropped off at least 2 days prior to your appointment for preparation. Ensure that your extensions are the correct type and quality for your desired style. If in doubt, please reach out for advice.

Human Hair Services

For hairstyles that require human hair, please DM me directly to discuss the available options. Pricing for human hair will vary based on the type and quantity needed.

Respect for Policies

These policies have been created to ensure a smooth and professional experience for both you and myself. Kindly respect these guidelines as you would any other professional service. I appreciate your understanding and cooperation.
